Think of it as three distinct layers of engagement:

  1. Visual Hierarchy (Elements): "How do I make this page look organized and scannable?"

  2. Active Engagement (Practices): "How do I stop learners from just skimming and make them think?"

  3. Media Richness: "How do I integrate video and images seamlessly?"

The Pedagogical Goal

The goal isn't just to make pages look "pretty." It is to increase "Time on Task." When a student has to click a tab, flip a card, or sort a list, they are mentally re-engaging with the material, which significantly improves retention compared to passive reading.
